Features ▾

EMAIL VERIFICATION

Mail Insight verifies sender domains and detects impersonation by comparing email sources against trusted domain records and known phishing patterns.

SCANNING LINKS AND IMAGES

It scans embedded links in emails, checks for mismatched URLs, and validates them using external threat intelligence services like Google Safe Browse.

RISK SCORING

Every email gets a risk score based on factors like the sender's history, message content, and known scams.

ALERT SYSTEM

MailInsight provides real-time pop-up warnings and personalized security recommendations based on the email's risk score to guide safe user actions.
